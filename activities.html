Our Activities

Discover the various activities and programs that keep our choir vibrant and engaged throughout the year.

Regular Activities

Weekly Rehearsals

Every Thursday & Sunday 18:00 - 20:00

Our dedicated choir members gather twice a week to practice hymns, learn new songs, and perfect our harmonies under the guidance of our choir director.

Learn More

Spiritual Formation

Every 2nd Saturday 10:00 - 12:00

Monthly sessions focused on deepening our faith through scripture study, prayer, and discussions about the spiritual dimension of our musical ministry.

Learn More

Voice Training Workshops

Monthly 09:00 - 13:00

Professional voice coaches conduct workshops to help members improve their vocal techniques, breathing exercises, and overall musical performance.

Learn More

Special Programs

Community Outreach

Quarterly Various Locations

We visit orphanages, hospitals, and prisons to share our music and bring hope to those in need through our ministry of song and fellowship.

Annual Retreat

August Weekend Event

A spiritual getaway combining prayer, reflection, team-building activities, and intensive music preparation for the coming liturgical year.
