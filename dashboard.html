About Voice of Ecclesia Zambia

Voice of Ecclesia Zambia (VOEZ) is a vibrant and dynamic Catholic musical choir that unites talented Catholic singers with a shared mission of inspiring faith, strengthening Christian unity, and uplifting communities through the power of music.

VOEZ was founded by a group of dedicated Catholic graduates, students, and non-students from various parishes across Zambia. Since its inception, the choir has grown into a recognized symbol of hope, joy, and devotion within the Catholic community. Through our carefully curated musical compositions, we seek to deepen the faith of believers, encourage worship, and foster a greater sense of togetherness among Christians.

Over the years, we have made significant contributions to Catholic evangelization through music. We have released two inspiring albums that reflect our devotion to God and our commitment to spreading His message. Our music has reached audiences far and wide through numerous television appearances on National Catholic Lumen TV, radio broadcasts, and live performances at various church events and religious gatherings.

In addition to our musical efforts, VOEZ is actively involved in outreach programs, visiting parishes across Zambia to minister through music and engage in community service. Our dedication extends beyond music, as we also prioritize entrepreneurship and charitable initiatives, aiming to uplift those in need and contribute to the well-being of society.
