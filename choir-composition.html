Our Choir Composition

The Voice of Ecclesia Zambia choir is composed of dedicated members who bring their unique talents and voices together to create harmonious worship experiences. Our choir is structured into traditional voice sections, each playing a vital role in our musical ministry.

Below you'll find information about our voice sections, their members, and the organizational structure that keeps our choir running smoothly.

Voice Sections

Soprano

The soprano section carries the melody with their higher vocal range. Our sopranos bring brightness and clarity to our musical pieces.

Section Members

  • Mary Chisanga Section Leader
  • Grace Mwila
  • Agness Banda
  • Beatrice Phiri
  • Ruth Mwale

Alto

The alto section provides rich harmonies with their lower female voices. They add depth and warmth to our choral sound.

Section Members

  • Esther Lungu Section Leader
  • Miriam Kabwe
  • Sarah Mwansa
  • Deborah Ngoma
  • Hannah Mwanza

Tenor

Our tenors bring the higher male voices that often carry the melody in harmony with the sopranos, creating beautiful musical blends.

Section Members

  • John Mwamba Section Leader
  • Peter Chanda
  • David Sichone
  • Joseph Mwape
  • Andrew Mulenga

Bass

The bass section provides the foundation of our choral sound with their deep, resonant tones that support all other voices.

Section Members

  • James Zulu Section Leader
  • Michael Bwalya
  • Daniel Mwila
  • Simon Kapata
  • Patrick Mwenda
