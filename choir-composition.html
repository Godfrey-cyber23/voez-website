Our Choir Composition

The Voice of Ecclesia Zambia choir is composed of dedicated members who bring their unique talents and voices together to create harmonious worship experiences. Our choir is structured with clear roles and responsibilities to ensure smooth operation and beautiful music ministry.

Roles & Responsibilities

CHIEF ADMINISTRATORS

Preside over matters not resolved by any of the Parties / Committees / Directors.

MUSIC DIRECTORS

Appoint conductors | Approve lyrics & composed tunes | Perform liturgical duties.

PROGRAMS DIRECTORS

Approve programs | Sign all letters drafted by Administrators / Committees.

SUB-GROUPS

Scout for members | Scout for programs | Membership fee | Admins will deliver Treasury duties.

VISITATION COMMITTEE

Organize visitations to Parishes / Sick members / Events for members (e.g. Wedding, Funeral).

BIBLE STUDY COMMITTEE

Ensure those on duty are ready to share readings in advance & afterwards, and prepare a summary.

UNIFORMS COMMITTEE

Suggest, approve & buy new uniforms | Ensure everyone has the right attire for each activity.

RADIO | TV SHOWS COMMITTEE

Schedule & ensure everyone appears on a radio/TV show at least once every 3 months. Approve scripts before a show | Guide newcomers | Provide high-quality content on social media.
